To assist in solving some of the issues that may arise as a result of the safety attire guidelines, alternatives are created. To help eliminate or lessen the health and safety issues for employees and customers all employees must wear safety attire before entering the floor. For employees who are non-compliant they will receive a warning the first time. Employees who are non-compliant the 2nd time must meet with the manager and receive another 2 points. A 3rd non-compliance will result in speaking with a manager, receive 2 points for non-compliance and will be sent home. A 4th non-compliance will result in 4 points and a 3-day suspension. Employees who are non-compliant 5 times may be terminated.
